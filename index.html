The Colour Wheel

Developed by Sir Isaac Newton, the colour wheel shows relationships between:

  • Primary colours: red, blue, yellow
  • Secondary colours: orange, green, purple
  • Tertiary colours: like red-orange or blue-green

Image from https://usabilitygeek.com/

Core Principles

  • Hue: Pure colour (e.g. blue or red)
  • Value: Lightness or darkness
  • Saturation: Intensity or vividness
  • Temperature: Warm or cool tones affecting mood

Image from https://usabilitygeek.com/

Colour Combinations

  • Complementary: Opposites that contrast strongly
  • Analogous: Neighbouring hues creating harmony
  • Triadic: Three evenly spaced colours offering balance
